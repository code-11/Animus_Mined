A space themed mining game by Brendan Ritter

Animus Mined is a 2-d space themed mining game made in Unity. You play as a NASA mining robot tasked with retreiving what scans indicate may be an alien artifact.

Along the way you will encounter hazards such as falling regolith, falling rocks, and falling... also water.

AM was inspired by games like minecraft, motherlode and robominer (for android).
